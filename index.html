the breeze carries an improvised symphony
from the lips of a quail into the lungs of mockingjay
eleven wound threads around the skies beds
leaves no elephant left in the room
two ships crossing paths in conflict they clash
lighting each other on fire
before they depart to contrary coasts
mariners exchanged fowls, two burning Schooners in the distance
two will keep whatever they seek as long as they give it away
weather underground or in the fists of millions
twin flames can start a revolution
